How to Download Your LinkedIn Profile as a PDF:

  1. Go to your LinkedIn profile.
  2. Click on the "More" button (three dots) located below your profile photo and headline.
  3. Select "Save to PDF" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Your profile will be downloaded as a PDF file. Upload this file here for analysis.
  5. Note: LinkedIn app does not support profile download in PDF. Log in to a browser and download.
